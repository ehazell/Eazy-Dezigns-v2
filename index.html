About Me

Ezra Hazell, is the CEO, Founder and Lead Designer of Eazy Dezigns, LLC and is responsible for creating new marketing materials and/or taking existing marketing materials up a notch. Ezra Hazell is highly skilled in graphics and interface design for websites, print collateral, social media platforms, flyers, and banners.

Ezra Hazell, also known by his friends as "Eazy", started learning about the Internet and began to teach himself how to use various software programs to become a well-advanced graphics designer and developer. With a background in computer technology and networking became a foundation for Ezra to use technology to bring his designs to life and use the world wide web as his canvas.

Ezra has a Bachelor's degree in Internet and Web Development and a Master's in Information Management from Arizona State University, along with an Associate's in Computer Networking. Ezra served in the United States Army as a Computer Information Systems Analyst and Network Engineer. Ezra did not want to hide the talent that he was blessed with and decided to share it with those who want to promote and expand their brand or organization.
